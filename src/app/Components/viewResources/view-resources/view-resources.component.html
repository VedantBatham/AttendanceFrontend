📺 Suggested Resource
  • Machine Learning (ML) is a field of computer science that focuses on teaching machines to learn from and make decisions or predictions based on data.
  • Its primary importance lies in its ability to enable computers to perform tasks that would normally require human intelligence.
  • Some of ML's historical milestones include Perceptrons, Neural Networks, along with pioneering works by Arthur Samuel, Alan Turing, and Frank Rosenblatt.
  • Data Preprocessing is the process of preparing data for analysis or ML algorithms such as filtering, cleaning, and transforming.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is a step that involves investigating and visualizing data to gain insights and understanding before building models.
  • Feature Selection is the process of selecting the most important features (variables) to include in a ML model for optimal performance.
